Baum is kinetic
Baum is human

Born from a series of sketches, Baum toes the line where tradition meets digital cleanliness, where human hand meets mathematics, where virtual meets tangible. Drawing inspiration from the blackletter, old-style faces like Janson, calligraphy, and digital visuals, Baum caters to use that looks forward and backwards.

Character Set

Baum features a full character set, complete with upper and lower cases, numerals, accented characters, symbols, ligatures, alternate stylistic sets, and contextual alternates.
